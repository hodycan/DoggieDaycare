Who we are?

Woof Entertainment is a group of students who worked together to create a simple game for dog lovers. Each member is in their last year at University of California, Irvine. Woof Entertainment is based out of Irvine, California and was founded in 2018 by the following members.

Cody Han is the Assistant Game Designer and Logo Designer.
